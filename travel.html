Hawaii

hawaii.gif

hawaii.jpg

Geographical Location: North America

Hawaii is a group of volcanic islands located in the central Pacific Ocean and it is the most isolated island chain on earth.

Hawaii is known for its law of the Aloha Spirit, the land of majestic beaches, hawaiian music, and hawaiian waterfalls.

Italy

italy.gif

italy.jpg

Geographical Location: Europe

Italy is a country located in Southern Europe comprising the boot-shaped Italian peninsula and a number of islands including Sicily and Sardinia.

Italy is well known for its architecture, culture, art, opera, literature, film, and fashion.

Taiwan

taiwan.gif

taiwan_101.jpg

Geographical Location: East Asia

Taiwan is a country in East Asia located at the junction of the East and South China Seas in the northwestern Pacific Ocean.

Taiwan is well known for their beef noodle soup, bubble tea, bicycles, convenience stores, and nightlife.
